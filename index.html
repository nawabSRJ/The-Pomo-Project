The Pomodoro App
Monitor your children

This is a Pomodoro Timer Clock System which also helps you to monitor your children by capturing their keyboard strokes and receive the data on your mail.This is solely developed for monitoring and educational purposes.Be careful and read the instructions before using the app and ensure you have filled the details correctly.


About Us

What do we do?

The Pomodoro Project aims at building systems for efficient monitoring and surveillance.Under this project we aim to build both software and hardware powered devices to help the users get rid of the tension they often suffer due to their absence.

Who are we?

We are a group of few people who have started this project with the mission to deliver our 100% in the service of the aim of our project and we will dedicate our time to this.
